Mulberry & Silkworm Nutrition Guidelines
Understand the importance of quality mulberry leaves and optimal feeding for silkworm growth and silk production.
🍃 Essential Feeding Principles for Silkworms
  • ✔️ Quality Mulberry Leaves: Provide fresh, tender, and pest-free leaves.
  • ✔️ Age-Appropriate Feeding: Adjust leaf maturity and quantity according to silkworm instars.
  • ✔️ Consistent Feeding Schedule: Maintain regular feeding times to ensure continuous growth.
  • ✔️ Avoid Over/Under-feeding: Overfeeding wastes leaves and degrades bedding; under-feeding starves worms.
  • ✔️ Proper Leaf Preservation: Store leaves correctly to maintain freshness and nutritional value.
🌿 Mulberry Leaf Stages & Types
Young Leaves (Ist & IInd Instar)
Tender, succulent leaves rich in moisture, ideal for young silkworms.
Mature Leaves (IIIrd & IVth Instar)
More fibrous leaves with balanced nutrients, suitable for middle-aged silkworms.
Hard Leaves (Vth Instar)
Fully grown, tougher leaves providing the necessary nutrients for the final growth stage.
Artificial Diet
A supplementary or alternative feed in paste form, offering controlled nutrition.

Crop Nutrition & Soil Guidelines
Understand the basic nutritional needs of your crops and soil for optimal growth and yield.
💧 Essential Crop Nutrition Principles
  • ✔️ Regular Soil Testing: Understand your soil's composition and nutrient levels.
  • ✔️ Balanced Fertilization: Apply nutrients (N-P-K) based on crop needs and soil test results.
  • ✔️ Efficient Water Management: Crucial for nutrient uptake and plant health; avoid over/under-watering.
  • ✔️ Promote Soil Organic Matter: Improves soil structure, water retention, and nutrient availability.
  • ✔️ Crop Rotation & Cover Cropping: Enhance soil health, suppress pests, and cycle nutrients naturally.
🪴 Common Soil Amendments & Nutrients
Organic Compost
Improves soil structure, adds diverse nutrients, and enhances microbial activity.
NPK Fertilizers
Provides essential Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium for robust plant growth.
Micronutrients
Trace elements like Iron, Zinc, Boron, critical for specific plant functions.
Cover Crops
Plants grown to protect and enrich the soil, not for harvest (e.g., legumes, grasses).
